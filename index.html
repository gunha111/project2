History of the Ninja

Origins of Ninjas

Ninjas, also known as Shinobi, were covert agents in feudal Japan. They specialized in espionage, guerrilla warfare, and assassination.

Skills & Tactics

Ninjas mastered stealth, disguise, and martial arts. They used shurikens, katanas, and various secret techniques.

Modern Influence

Today, ninjas are popular in movies, games, and anime. Their legacy continues in modern martial arts and pop culture.
